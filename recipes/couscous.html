Couscous

The quintessential North African dish

Couscous is an iconic North African dish of steamed wheat semolina. It's versatile, healthy, and served with meat, vegetables, and spices.

Whether enjoyed as a side dish or a main course, couscous is a delicious and filling addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb stew meat
  • 6 cups couscous fine semolina
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons harissa
  • 2 tablespoons ground caraway
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 turnips
  • 6 medium carrots
  • 4 zucchini
  • 6 small potatoes
  • 10 oz. chickpeas
  • 4 green hot peppers ,
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, fry onions for 2 mins.
  2. Add garlic, tomato paste, and harissa, fry for 2 more mins.
  3. Add caraway, coriander, and chili powder, fry for another 2 mins.
  4. Add lamb meat, cook for 5 mins stirring regularly.
  5. Cover with water, add turnips and carrots, cook for 45 mins on low/medium heat.
  6. Prepare couscous as directed.
  7. After 45 mins, add zucchini, potatoes, and chickpeas.
  8. Cook for 30-40 mins on low/medium heat.
  9. 10 mins before end of cooking, add broth to couscous, mix well.
  10. Arrange couscous in a tajine pot, place cooked vegetables and meat around it. Optionally, garnish with fried green hot peppers.
